Welcome to Debate Synergy

Debate Synergy is a web app for high school and college policy & LD debate teams to store online research, discuss debate in the forums, and livestream debate rounds. Featured in Wired magazine.

Download Debate Sidebar Word AddIn on Windows

Manual

Sign in with Google to create your account and access your Google Drive files.
Basic formatting buttons: heading, read, read card, super read card, remove formatting. These can be used on the text you copy from webpages or paste from Word documents. F1-F6 keyboard shortcuts, also standards like Ctrl+B bold, Ctrl+U underline, Ctrl+Z undo, Ctrl+C copy, Ctrl+V paste
Create new file button to make new files visible in the file tree, or import a file from Google Drive. Drop Word documents on to the browser to import them.
Configure settings and create custom js/css plugins that enhance the interface for only you.
Search all your files by headings or by full text.
The file tree has extensive features: click to open file or go to heading, double click file to show rename/delete options, double click header to select block, selects current block automatically as you scroll, collapsible file contents, and drag and drop headings to rearrange them. Heading levels 1, 2, 3 are recognized automatically from document and tabbed.
Use back / forward buttons in browser or on mouse (or Alt+Right/Left) to go to previously opened and next files in your docs.
Show the round tab to start rounds and see history. Invite the debaters and judges to join the round room. After sending out invites to other users by their registered email with Google sign-in, they must click the alert to accept.
Drag and drop from your docs to copy duplicate of selection into any of the speech tab titles or in a given position in current speech.
After your speech is ready, publish it for opponents to have it streamed to them up to the position that you have scrolled to, with live updating.
Make round panel full screen, or show speeches side-by-side with prior after the 1NC, so you can be creating your response speech while watching your opponents cards being live streamed.
Toggle font sizes, to show either: normal font sizes, bold and underlined text larger, and bold and highlighted text larger.
The timer keeps track of prep and can be manually set.
Works well on Chrome / Firefox on laptop & mobile. Mobile swipe to show/hide sidebar.
